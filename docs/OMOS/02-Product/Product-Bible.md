## Purpose

The Product Bible is the master reference for what OneMember is, who it serves, and how every product area works. It is the most important document in OMOS.

Every sprint spec, architecture decision, and brand guideline must be consistent with the Product Bible. When there is a conflict, the Product Bible wins — unless the Product Owner explicitly approves an exception and updates this document.

---

## Structure (To Be Completed in Sprint AI-02B)

The Product Bible will cover:

### 1. Product Overview
- What OneMember is in one paragraph
- What it is not (explicit non-goals)
- Primary user personas (merchants, staff, customers, enterprises)
- The core user journey for each persona

### 2. Merchant Platform
- Loyalty programme engine (points, stamps, tiers)
- Member management
- Campaign and reward management
- Dashboard and analytics
- Settings and customisation
- Subscription and billing

### 3. Customer Wallet
- Universal loyalty wallet concept
- Join-by-QR flow
- Privacy and consent model
- Cross-brand loyalty view
- Notifications and promotions

### 4. Enterprise Bridge
- What the enterprise bridge is and who it serves
- Bridge API specification
- White-label mode
- Enterprise onboarding

### 5. Commerce
- Product and menu listing
- Order types: dine-in, pickup, delivery, shipping
- Delivery radius and fee configuration
- Direct merchant commerce (vs platform commerce)

### 6. POS (Point of Sale Lite)
- Target users (small businesses without a POS)
- Core POS flows: record sale, award points, issue receipt
- Staff-facing interface
- Counter Mode

### 7. Inventory
- Basic inventory tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Integration with POS and commerce

### 8. Procurement
- *(Future phase — placeholder)*

### 9. Accounting
- *(Future phase — placeholder)*

### 10. Analytics
- Merchant analytics (campaign performance, member engagement)
- Privacy-first customer analytics (consent-based, anonymised)
- Admin/platform analytics

### 11. AI Features
- Merchant Intelligence (existing: health score, insights, opportunities)
- Future: AI-driven campaign recommendations
- Future: Anomaly detection on member activity
