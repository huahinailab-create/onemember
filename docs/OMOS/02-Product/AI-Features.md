AI Features will cover:

### Existing (Merchant Intelligence — Sprint 6.7)
- Business health score (0–100, four categories)
- AI-generated insights (pattern detection in member and transaction data)
- Opportunity detection (low redemption rate, birthday members not targeted, etc.)

### Planned
- AI-driven campaign recommendations (suggested offer based on member behaviour)
- Smart member segmentation (AI-classified cohorts: high-value, at-risk, new)
- Anomaly detection (unusual transaction patterns, potential fraud)
- Natural language query for merchant analytics ('How many members visited this month?')

### Principles for AI Features
- AI suggestions are always advisory — the merchant decides
- No AI model is trained on data from one merchant to benefit another
- All AI outputs are explainable (show why a recommendation was made)
