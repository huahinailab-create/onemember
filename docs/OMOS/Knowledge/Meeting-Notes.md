## Purpose

Notes from significant product, strategy, or stakeholder meetings. Provides context for decisions made outside the formal sprint process.

---

## How to Use This File

Record notes from significant meetings here — product reviews, strategy sessions, investor conversations, merchant advisory discussions. Notes that capture WHY a decision was made are more valuable than notes that only capture WHAT was decided (the decision itself goes in CEO-Decisions.md or CTO-Decisions.md).

Format:
```
### MTG-[NNN] — [Meeting Title]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Attendees:** [Roles, not necessarily names]
**Context:** [What brought this meeting about]
**Key Discussion Points:**
- [Point 1]
**Decisions Made:**
- [Decision 1 — link to CEO-Decisions.md or CTO-Decisions.md if formal]
**Action Items:**
- [ ] [Action] — Owner: [Role] — Due: YYYY-MM-DD
```
