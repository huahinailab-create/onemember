# 03 — Home Page (section-by-section)

> The home page is the street demo for people we'll never meet. Ten sections, one scroll, phone-first. Copy below is the English master; Thai is written natively from the same intent (never literal translation). [Layout] notes are creative direction, not dev specs.

---

## 1 — Hero

- **Headline:** *Your regulars, coming back more often.*
- **Sub-headline:** OneMember turns one-time customers into loyal regulars — set up in 10 minutes, free for your first 100 members. No hardware. No app downloads.
- **Body:** none (hero stays clean).
- **Primary CTA:** `Start Free` → register. Micro-copy beneath: *Free up to 100 members · no credit card · 10-minute setup.*
- **Secondary CTA:** `▶ Watch the 90-second demo` (opens video overlay).
- **Visual:** photo-real: Thai café counter, QR poster beside the till, customer scanning with a phone, owner smiling mid-pour. Real shop, real light — no stocky gloss, no 3D blobs. Mobile: owner + poster crop.
- **Trust elements:** slim strip under CTA — "Trusted by [n] shops across Thailand" (hidden until n≥20) · Thai flag + "ภาษาไทย 100%" · PDPA-ready badge (post-DR-33).
- **[Layout]:** full-viewport on mobile; copy left / photo right on desktop; pink CTA on navy-tinted photo overlay.

## 2 — Problem ("the quiet Tuesday")

- **Headline:** *You know your bestsellers. Do you know your best customers?*
- **Sub-headline:** Most shops lose regulars silently — no goodbye, no complaint. They just stop coming.
- **Body (3 pain cards):** ① *The lost card* — "Another full stamp card 'lost'. Another free coffee to a stranger." ② *The silent goodbye* — "Your Tuesday regular hasn't come in three weeks. Nobody noticed." ③ *Renting customers* — "Delivery apps bring orders — and keep the customer, the data, and 30%."
- **CTA:** none (problem sections don't sell; they nod).
- **Visual:** three illustrated cards in brand style — warm, slightly witty line illustrations (crumpled stamp card; empty café chair; delivery bag with a price tag).
- **[Layout]:** 3 cards, horizontal desktop / stacked mobile.

## 3 — Solution ("how it works" — mirrors the street demo)

- **Headline:** *Big-chain loyalty, sized for your shop.*
- **Sub-headline:** Three steps. Ten minutes. No IT person required.
- **Body (3 steps):** **1. Put the poster on your counter** — customers scan, join in 15 seconds, no app to download. **2. Two taps at the till** — staff record a purchase; points and stamps add up automatically. **3. Watch them come back** — OneMember spots who's drifting away and helps you win them back.
- **Primary CTA:** `Start Free`. **Secondary:** `See how it works for your industry ↓` (anchors to §5).
- **Visual:** 3-frame sequence: poster scan → Counter Mode two taps (real UI screenshot in phone frame) → dashboard win-back alert (real UI). Real product screens = trust.
- **Trust:** caption under frame 2 — "Staff learn it in one coffee."

- **Visual:** icon + one product-screen sliver per tile; consistent bi-icon style already in the design system.

- Performance target: hero readable <1.5s on 4G (see [13 Launch Checklist](./13-Launch-Checklist.md)).
