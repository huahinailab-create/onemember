# 05 — Industries (10 landing pages)

> These are our highest-converting SEO pages: a shop owner searching "ระบบสะสมแต้ม ร้านกาแฟ" lands on a page that *sounds like her shop*. One template, ten renditions — problems in the owner's words, help as outcomes, campaigns from [SALES-001 §04](../Sales/04-Industry-Playbooks.md), a success-story slot, one CTA.
>
> **Template:** Hook headline → "Sound familiar?" (3 problems) → "How OneMember helps" (3 outcomes) → "Campaigns that work for [industry]" (recipe box) → Success story `[placeholder]` → industry FAQ (3 Qs) → CTA banner. ~400 words + real photos of that shop type.
