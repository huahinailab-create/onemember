# 04 — Features (outcome pages)

> Iron rule: **describe business outcomes, never software.** Every feature page follows one template and answers one merchant question: *"what does my shop get?"*
>
> **Template per page:** Outcome headline → "the old way vs. with OneMember" → 3 benefit blocks (each: merchant sentence + real screenshot) → mini-story placeholder → industry cross-links → Start Free CTA. ~350–500 words. No settings lists, no jargon.

---

## /features/members — *Know every regular by name*
- **Headline:** Know every regular by name — even with 500 of them.
- **Old way / new way:** "Your best customers live in your memory (and leave with your staff)." → "Every customer who scans your poster becomes a member you can see, message, and reward."
- **Benefits:** ① Every member's visits, points and birthday in one place — the notebook that writes itself. ② See who's new, who's loyal, who's drifting. ③ The list is *yours* — export it any day, take it anywhere.
- **Proof placeholder:** `[STORY: salon that saved its client book when a stylist left]`

## /features/campaigns — *Points or stamps — your rules*
- **Headline:** Reward every visit — points or stamps, your rules.
- **Old way:** rubber stamp, lost cards, no rules. → **New:** "Buy 9 get 1 free" or "1 point per ฿100" — set once, runs itself.
- **Benefits:** ① Launch your first campaign in minutes with industry presets. ② Change the rules any time — customers see it instantly. ③ Birthday rewards send themselves — you get the thank-you.
- **Proof:** `[STORY: café killing dead afternoons with double stamps]`

## /features/rewards — *Give them a reason to return*
- **Headline:** Give customers a reason to come back this week.
- **Benefits:** ① Rewards your shop can afford — an upsize, an add-on, a free coffee. ② Ladders that keep the next goal in sight ("2 stamps from a free drink" beats "we exist"). ③ Two taps to redeem — the happiest moment at your counter, made simple.
- **Proof:** `[STORY: nail salon whose 6-stamp reward lifted rebooking]`

## /features/commerce — *Sell more, commission-free*
- **Headline:** Take direct orders. Keep 100% of them.
- **Old way:** "Every delivery-app order costs you ~30% — and they keep the customer." → **New:** your own product catalogue and orders, at 0% commission, with every buyer becoming *your* member.
- **Benefits:** ① Your menu/products online in an afternoon. ② Orders straight to you — you confirm, you fulfil, you keep the margin. ③ Customers pay you the way they already do (your PromptPay QR).
- **Proof:** `[STORY: restaurant moving regulars from apps to direct]`
