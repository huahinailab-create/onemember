## 💰 Pricing (1–12)

1. **Is the Free plan really free?** Yes — up to 100 members, forever. No card, no trial expiry, no catch.
2. **What happens when I reach 100 members?** Congratulations — you have 100 regulars! Your existing members keep working; to add more, upgrade to a paid plan.
3. **What happens when my 30-day Professional trial ends?** You choose: pick a paid plan or drop to Free. You keep all your data either way.
4. **Do I need a credit card to start?** No. You only enter payment details if you choose a paid plan. ⚖️
5. **Can I cancel anytime?** Yes, from your settings, effective end of the billing period. Your data stays exportable.
6. **Do you take a percentage of my sales?** Never. Flat monthly plans only — your revenue is yours.
7. **What payment methods can I use to pay OneMember?** Card today; PromptPay is on our roadmap for Thai merchants. ⚖️
8. **Is there a discount for paying yearly?** `[Per DECISION-014 — planned framing: 2 months free]` ⚖️
9. **Do prices include VAT?** `[Per DECISION-014 / tax registration status]` ⚖️
10. **What happens to my data if I stop paying?** Nothing bad — your account drops to Free limits and your data waits for you. Export it any time.
11. **Can I change plans later?** Yes — up or down, any time, from settings.
12. **Is there a refund policy?** See our refund policy `[/legal/refunds — pending DR-33 legal pass]`. ⚖️

## 🚀 Setup (13–24)

13. **How long does setup really take?** About 10 minutes: shop details, first campaign, first reward, print your poster. The app guides you step by step.
14. **Do I need any hardware?** No. Your phone (or any browser) is enough. The poster is just paper.
15. **Do my customers need to download an app?** No — they scan your QR poster and join in about 15 seconds in their browser.
16. **Do I need a website first?** No. OneMember even gives you one (your storefront page).
17. **Will it work with my POS?** Yes — OneMember runs beside any POS. Staff record loyalty in Counter Mode in two taps; your POS keeps doing payments.
18. **How do my staff learn it?** Two taps: find the member, confirm. There's a printable one-page Thai staff guide in your Launch Kit.
19. **How do I get my first members?** Put the poster by your till and have staff ask one question: "สะสมแต้มไหมคะ?" Most shops get 20–50 members in week one.
20. **Can I import my existing customer list?** Yes — import members from a spreadsheet (name, phone, birthday).
21. **Can I use it on iPhone and Android?** Yes — it runs in the browser on anything, and installs to your home screen like an app.
22. **What if I get stuck?** Every screen has a ? button with a Thai guide, and we answer on LINE fast.
23. **Can I try it before putting it in my shop?** Yes — sign up free and play; nothing goes live to customers until your poster is on the counter.
24. **Can I run it for two shops?** Multi-branch management is an Enterprise feature — talk to us. A second independent shop can simply have its own account.

## 🔒 Security & Data (25–33)

25. **Who owns my customer data?** You do. Fully, always, on every plan.
26. **Can I export my member list?** Yes — any time, in a standard spreadsheet format.
27. **Will you sell or share my customer data?** No. Never. It's your list, not our product.
28. **Is OneMember PDPA-compliant?** Consent is captured when customers join, and data rights are built in. `[Full statement pending DR-33 legal review]` ⚖️
29. **Where is my data stored?** On secure cloud infrastructure with encrypted connections and daily backups. `[Region statement per ops]`
30. **Can my staff see everything?** Staff use Counter Mode for daily work; account settings and exports stay with the owner login. (Granular roles: Enterprise.)
31. **What happens if I delete a member?** They're archived from your list; you can restore them. Permanent deletion requests are honored per PDPA. ⚖️
32. **Can customers see other customers' data?** No — a member sees only their own points, rewards and history.
33. **What if OneMember shuts down?** Your list is exportable every single day — you are never locked in. (And we're building for the long haul.)

## ⭐ Loyalty (34–45)

34. **Points or stamps — which should I choose?** Stamps for visit-based shops (coffee, nails), points for varied spend (restaurants, salons, retail). Our industry guides recommend a setup for your type.
35. **Can I run points and stamps at the same time?** One active campaign type keeps things simple for customers; you can change type whenever you like.
36. **How do customers earn?** Staff record the visit/purchase in Counter Mode — two taps — and points or stamps land instantly.
37. **Can customers see their points?** Yes — their member page shows points, stamps, and how close the next reward is.
38. **What rewards work best?** Small and reachable beats big and distant: an upsize at 4 stamps beats a free meal at 50. Industry pages have proven recipes.
39. **Can rewards run out?** Yes — you can limit quantity (e.g. first 20 redemptions) or leave them unlimited.
40. **How does the birthday reward work?** Set it once; members with a birthday coming get their reward automatically. You just enjoy the smiles.
41. **What is a win-back alert?** OneMember spots regulars who haven't visited in a while (you pick the days) and lists them — one LINE broadcast brings them home.
42. **Can points expire?** Yes, optionally — expiry creates a gentle "use them this month" reason to visit.
43. **Can I adjust a member's points manually?** Yes — with a note, so there's always a record.
44. **Can customers cheat the system?** Staff record every earn at the counter, so points only move when your team says so. Rewards redeem with a tap in front of staff.
45. **Do memberships work across different shops?** Each shop's programme is its own. (A shared network across shops is our long-term vision — not a today feature.)

## 🛒 Commerce (46–52)

46. **What is the Commerce feature?** Your products online, orders coming straight to you — with 0% commission.
47. **Do you charge per order?** No. Flat plan, zero commission, forever.
48. **How do I get paid for orders?** Customers pay you directly, the way they already do — e.g. your PromptPay QR or cash on pickup. Money never passes through OneMember.
49. **Does OneMember handle delivery?** No — you fulfil pickup/delivery as you do today; OneMember handles the ordering and the customer relationship.
50. **Can I limit stock?** Yes — set stock per product, and it shows sold-out automatically.
51. **Do orders earn loyalty points?** Customers who order become members, and staff can record the purchase for points like any visit.
52. **Is Commerce right for my shop?** Best for restaurants, cafés, retail and fashion; service shops often skip it — and that's fine, it's optional.

## 💳 Payments (53–58)

53. **Does OneMember process customer payments?** No — by design. Customers pay you directly; we never touch, hold, or take a cut of your money.
54. **Can I show my PromptPay QR at checkout?** Yes — upload it once and it appears on your storefront orders.
55. **Do you support credit-card payments from customers?** Customers pay you by whatever you accept today. OneMember doesn't sit in the middle.
56. **How do I pay my OneMember subscription?** Card today; local options for Thailand are on the roadmap. ⚖️
57. **Will you support Myanmar payment apps (KBZPay/WavePay)?** For customer payments — yes, effectively already: upload your payment QR, any provider. For subscription billing in Myanmar, we'll announce options with our launch there.
58. **Is there an invoice for my subscription?** Yes — receipts are emailed and available in settings. ⚖️

## 🏬 Storefront (59–65)

59. **What is my storefront?** Your shop's own page — logo, story, products, loyalty and the join button — at your own link.
60. **Can I choose my storefront address?** Yes — set your Store URL in settings (onemember.co/store/**your-name**).
61. **Can I use my own domain?** Not yet — it's on the Enterprise roadmap. Your OneMember link works beautifully on LINE/IG bios today.
62. **Does the storefront work in Thai?** Yes — and you choose which languages your customers see.
63. **Can I change how it looks?** Your logo, colors and content — yes. Full white-label design is an Enterprise feature.
64. **Do I need photography?** Phone photos in good light work great; our guides show you how to make products look delicious.
65. **What if I don't want a public page?** Skip it — the loyalty poster and counter work fully without a storefront.
