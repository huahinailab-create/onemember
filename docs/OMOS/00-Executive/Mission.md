## Purpose

This document defines the mission of OneMember — what the company does every day in service of its vision.

The mission is operational where the vision is aspirational. It answers: "What do we do, for whom, and why does it matter right now?"

## Notes for AI-02B

The mission statement must:
- Be specific to what OneMember actually does (not generic SaaS language)
- Name the customer (SMB merchants in Southeast Asia)
- Reference the key value delivered (loyalty, ownership, direct commerce)
- Be short enough to remember without reading it

Distinguish clearly from the vision:
- Vision = the world we want to create (aspirational, 10-year horizon)
- Mission = what we do today to get there (operational, present tense)
