## Purpose

## Thailand Market Overview

### SME Landscape
Thailand has approximately **3.2 million registered SMEs** (as of 2024 data), with the large majority being micro and small businesses. Key sectors for OneMember:

| Sector | Estimated Businesses | OneMember Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| Food and Beverage | 500,000+ | **Critical** — highest repeat visit frequency |
| Retail | 400,000+ | **High** — points drive larger baskets |
| Beauty and Wellness | 150,000+ | **High** — repeat visits, high client value |
| Hospitality | 30,000+ | **Medium** — boutique hotels, guesthouses |
| Other services | 200,000+ | **Medium** — fitness, education, auto |

**Total addressable market (Thailand, Phase 1):** Approximately 1.2 million SME merchants for whom repeat customer management is a core business need.

**Initial serviceable market (Phase 1 focus):** Merchants in Bangkok and major urban areas with digital payment adoption. Estimate: 200,000–300,000 businesses.

**Realistic near-term target (Year 1–2):** 1,000–5,000 active paying merchants. Proof of concept for the flywheel.

## Revenue Opportunity

### Per-Merchant Revenue
At 1,000 THB average monthly subscription, 5,000 merchants = 5M THB ARR from subscriptions alone.

At 50,000 merchants (Year 5 target), subscription ARR = 50M–100M THB depending on plan mix.

Commerce transaction fees (Phase 3) and Enterprise Bridge fees (Phase 2) represent additional revenue streams that scale with merchant and transaction volume.

### Customer Wallet Network Effect
The Customer Wallet (Phase 2) creates a consumer-side network that makes OneMember more valuable for every new merchant who joins. At 50,000 merchants with an average of 500 active members each, the OneMember network reaches 25 million member-merchant relationships — a significant data and distribution asset.

## Regional Expansion Opportunity

OneMember's platform is designed to expand regionally. The initial markets after Thailand:

| Market | Timeline | Key Localisation |
|---|---|---|
| Malaysia | Phase 4 | Malay language, DuitNow payments, Malaysia PDPA |
| Vietnam | Phase 4 | Vietnamese language, VNPay, local accounting |
| Singapore | Phase 4+ | English-first, PayNow, high competition |

Southeast Asia has approximately 70 million SMEs across the region (World Bank estimate). Even a 0.1% penetration represents 70,000 merchants — a significant business.

## Competitive Landscape

| Category | Players | OneMember Position |
|---|---|---|
| Global enterprise loyalty | Salesforce Loyalty, Comarch, Annex Cloud | Not competitive — price and complexity |
| Regional loyalty apps | Stamp Me, Perx, Loyalty Gator | Competitive — OneMember has deeper platform |
| Local Thai solutions | Custom LineOA, paper, ad-hoc apps | Directly competitive — OneMember wins on features |
| Point-of-sale platforms | Loyverse, Square Loyalty | Adjacent — OneMember may integrate |

OneMember's primary competition is **inertia** — the merchant doing nothing, or using paper cards. Converting merchants from "nothing" to OneMember is the primary growth opportunity.
