# 10 — Sales KPIs

> Numbers per full-time seller unless marked (team). Founder pro-rates by selling days. Until a CRM tool exists, the CRM **sheet** is the single source of truth — an unlogged activity didn't happen.
> North-star chain: **Demos → Trials → Activated → Retained → Referring.** Revenue follows activation; activation follows demos.

## Daily (logged same-day, reviewed each morning)

| Metric | Target | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| New prospects spoken to | 10 | Real conversation with owner/manager (not leaflet drops) |
| Demos delivered | 3–4 | Full 10-minute script, decision-maker present |
| Trials started | 1–2 | Signup completed (checklist begun) |
| Follow-ups completed | 100% of due | Per [08](./08-Follow-Up-Playbook.md) cadences |
| CRM hygiene | 100% | Every touch logged; every account has a next-touch date |

## Weekly (Friday review, 30 min, against the funnel)

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Demos | 15–20 | **The** leading indicator — protect it from admin creep |
| Demo → trial rate | ≥50% | Below 40% two weeks running = demo script problem, retrain |
| Trials Launch Ready ≤7 days | ≥70% | Activation is a sales metric here — we sign up merchants in person |
| New members added across my merchants | trend ↑ | Their success is the product we sell next door |
| Referral names collected | 3+ | Asked at every success touch |
| At-risk accounts flagged | all | Day-7 not-launched, silent trials, usage drops |

## Monthly (team review with PO/CTO)

| Metric | Target (first-100 phase) | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Leads** (qualified prospects) | 120+ | ICP-fit, decision-maker identified |
| **Meetings/demos** | 60+ | |
| **Trials** | 25+ | |
| **Activation** | ≥70% | Checklist 100% within 14 days of signup |
| **Conversion** — trial→paid | ≥25% | At trial end (day 30) |
| Conversion — trial→active (paid **or** Free-transacting) | ≥60% | Free-active is a win, tracked separately |
| **Retention** | <5% monthly logo churn (paid); ≥60% WAU/MAU usage | WAU = ≥1 transaction that week |
| **Referral share** | ≥25% of new trials | Source field mandatory in CRM |
| **Revenue** | MRR vs DECISION-014 plan; ARPU tracked from merchant #21 | Pilot cohort excluded from revenue KPIs |
| Time-to-value | ≤7 days median | Signup → first real member transaction |
| Free→paid graduation | ≥10%/quarter of Free merchants ≥80 members | The 100-member wall working |

## Funnel math (sanity check the targets hang together)

```
10 prospects/day → ~3.5 demos → ~1.75 trials → ×70% activation ≈ 1.2 activated/day
→ ~25 activated/month → ×25% paid + Free-active pool
≈ 100 activated merchants in ~4 months with ONE seller — matches GO-TO-MARKET-001.
```
If any stage underperforms two weeks running, fix **that stage** (script, follow-up, targeting) before adding volume.

## Health & anti-gaming rules

- A demo without the decision-maker counts as a *conversation*, not a demo.
- A trial that never reaches 20% checklist within 48h counts against demo quality, not just activation.
- Never sign up a merchant who can't name who will run Counter Mode — vanity trials poison every downstream number.
- Churn review: every cancelled/lapsed merchant gets a logged reason (verbatim) — reasons roll up to CTO monthly.

## Review rhythm

| Cadence | Ritual |
|---|---|
| Daily (10 min, morning) | Yesterday's numbers vs. targets; today's cluster + due follow-ups |
| Weekly (Fri, 30 min) | Funnel table above; one improvement action for next week — one, not five |
| Monthly (60 min, with PO/CTO) | Full KPI table; churn reasons; pricing/product feedback; targets re-forecast |
| Quarterly | Comp/quota review once pricing (DECISION-014) and first hires exist |
