# 04 — Industry Playbooks

> One recipe per industry: which mechanics to configure, in what order, and the quick win that makes the merchant feel smart in week one.
> Mechanics legend — **Points**: earn per ฿ spent · **Stamps**: earn per visit/service · **Rewards**: what points/stamps buy · **Birthday**: automated birthday reward (built) · **Referral**: manual "bring a friend" reward pattern (configured as a reward; no dedicated module yet — never promise automation) · **Seasonal**: limited-time double-earn or special reward.

**Universal launch sequence (all industries — mirrors the in-app checklist):** profile + logo → campaign → first reward → 10 founding members (staff + best regulars, day one) → QR poster printed & displayed → staff shown Counter Mode (2 minutes) → first win-back review at week 3.

---

## ☕ Coffee Shop
- **Mechanic:** Stamps — "buy 9, get 1 free" (digital version of what customers already understand).
- **Rewards:** free drink (9 stamps); upsize (4 stamps) as a fast first reward.
- **Birthday:** free pastry — costs little, feels big.
- **Referral:** "friend's first coffee −50%" reward redeemed by the referrer.
- **Seasonal:** double stamps 14:00–17:00 (kills the dead afternoon); rainy-day double stamps.
- **Quick win:** poster at the register + cashier asks "สะสมแต้มไหมคะ?" — 30+ members in week one from foot traffic alone.

## 🍜 Restaurant
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿100 (tickets vary too much for stamps).
- **Rewards:** ladder — free appetizer (low), free main (mid), ฿300 off family meal (high).
- **Birthday:** free dessert for the table (brings a group, not a person).
- **Seasonal:** weekday-lunch double points; Songkran/New Year specials.
- **+ Storefront:** publish menu for direct orders — "every direct order saves you the app commission."
- **Quick win:** win-back alert on 30-day absentees → one LINE message → tables that "came back from the dead" week 3.

## 💇 Hair Salon
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿ (coloring vs cut varies) — or stamps if menu is flat.
- **Rewards:** free treatment add-on (5 visits), free cut (10), premium product (high tier).
- **Birthday:** blowout or treatment upgrade.
- **Referral:** both sides get a treatment add-on.
- **Seasonal:** pre-Songkran / pre-New Year "get ready" double points.
- **Quick win:** win-back at 60 days — "we miss you" + small reward; overdue clients rebook within days. **Sequence note:** enter the member with notes (color formula!) during checkout — the notes alone hook owner-stylists.

## 💅 Nail Salon
- **Mechanic:** Stamps per visit — flat, frequent, perfect fit.
- **Rewards:** free basic mani (6 stamps), free gel (10), nail-art upgrade (4, fast win).
- **Birthday:** free nail-art add-on.
- **Referral:** bring-a-friend → both get an add-on (nails are social — this fires naturally).
- **Seasonal:** collection-linked designs for members only.
- **Quick win:** enroll every client in the chair this week (captive 45 minutes) — 60%+ of the book becomes members in 14 days.

## 💆 Massage & Spa
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿ (60/90/120-min prices differ).
- **Rewards:** +30 minutes free (mid), free 60-min session (high), oil upgrade (low/fast).
- **Birthday:** aroma upgrade or +15 min.
- **Seasonal:** low-season (May–Sep weekday) double points for locals.
- **Quick win:** separate locals from tourists in week one — finally answers "how many actual regulars do we have?"; win-back the 45-day locals.

## 🏨 Hotel (boutique)
- **Mechanic:** Points on room nights (booked direct) + F&B spend.
- **Rewards:** late checkout (free to give!), room upgrade, spa/F&B credit.
- **Birthday:** welcome amenity on next stay.
- **Seasonal:** green-season direct-booking double points.
- **Sequence note:** start with F&B + returning-guest desk flow; do NOT wait for PMS dreams.
- **Quick win:** front desk enrolls checkouts with "your next direct booking earns…" — measurable OTA bypass in a month.

## 🛍️ Retail
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿50–100 depending on ticket.
- **Rewards:** ฿ discounts at clear thresholds; member-only weekly item.
- **Birthday:** fixed ฿ voucher for the month.
- **Seasonal:** point-expiry pressure ("points expire Dec 31 — spend them"), double-point weekends.
- **Quick win:** basket-size bump — cashier says "you're ฿40 from your next 10 points"; watch average ticket move in 2–3 weeks.

## 🐕 Pet Shop
- **Mechanic:** Stamps for grooming + points for retail (two campaigns if volume justifies; grooming stamps first).
- **Rewards:** free nail trim (4), free groom (8); retail: food discount tiers.
- **Birthday:** **pet birthday** — treat bag + photo moment (lead with this in the pitch; owners melt).
- **Referral:** puppy-friend voucher.
- **Seasonal:** summer-cut season double stamps.
- **Quick win:** pet birthday reward posted by delighted owners on Facebook = free local marketing loop.

## 💉 Beauty Clinic
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿1,000 (high tickets).
- **Rewards:** free add-on (mask/LED), course-completion bonus treatment, high-tier discount on next course.
- **Birthday:** premium add-on during birthday month.
- **Referral:** formalize the existing word-of-mouth — both sides get treatment credit (their #1 acquisition channel, now measurable).
- **Seasonal:** wedding-season packages for members.
- **Quick win:** course-completion nudge — points visibly accumulating toward the completion bonus lifts show-up rate on sessions 4–6.

## 👗 Fashion
- **Mechanic:** Points per ฿; member tiers via reward ladder.
- **Rewards:** ฿ vouchers, **early access to new drops (members shop 24h before IG post)** — costs nothing, feels exclusive.
- **Birthday:** birthday-month discount.
- **Referral:** style-twin voucher for both.
- **Seasonal:** collection-launch double points weekend.
- **Quick win:** next drop announced to members first via LINE → visible spike proves "followers we own beat followers we rent."
