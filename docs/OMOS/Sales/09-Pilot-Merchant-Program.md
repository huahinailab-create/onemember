# 09 — Pilot Merchant Program ("Founding Merchants")

> Refines GO-TO-MARKET-001 §10 into an operating manual. The pilot is not a discount program — it is our **proof factory**: it must output activated merchants, case studies, testimonials, referrals, and a bug-free-enough product, in 8 weeks.

## Goals (program-level, in priority order)

1. **Prove activation:** ≥15/20 merchants Launch Ready ≤7 days and ≥50 members by week 4.
2. **Manufacture proof:** ≥5 case studies, ≥3 video testimonials, ≥10 referral names.
3. **Harden the product:** every pilot friction logged, synthesized fortnightly to CTO (bugs > friction > wishes).
4. **Calibrate the pitch:** demo script and ROI defaults updated with real numbers by week 8.

## Cohort design

20 merchants: 8 coffee · 5 hair/nail · 4 restaurant · 3 retail/pet/other; ≥5 outside Bangkok; personas mostly P1 with 2–3 P3 (multi-branch) for the Enterprise narrative. Recruit in 3 weeks via street sales — pilot invitations are *earned in the demo*, not advertised.

## The exchange (say it exactly like this)

| Founding Merchants receive | Founding Merchants commit to |
|---|---|
| 6 months **Professional free** (then standard pricing — say so upfront) | Weekly 15-min feedback call (LINE), 8 weeks |
| "Founding Merchant" plaque + digital badge | Displaying the QR poster prominently |
| Direct founder LINE (business hours <2h response) | Letting us use their logo + anonymized numbers |
| First access to new features | A testimonial *if — and only if — satisfied* |
| Their story told (free marketing for them) | An honest exit interview if they quit |

**Expectations we hold ourselves to:** we do the onboarding *with* them (not for them — we're testing the self-serve journey); we never publish anything without written (LINE) consent; we fix pilot-reported bugs before feature work.

## Weekly review process (per merchant, 15 min, LINE call)

Fixed agenda — same 4 beats every week so trends are comparable:
1. **Numbers** (we bring them): members, transactions, redemptions, win-back sends vs. last week.
2. **The Question:** *"What almost made you stop using it this week?"* — asked verbatim, every week. This one question is the program.
3. One thing they tried (or didn't) from their [industry playbook](./04-Industry-Playbooks.md).
4. Next week's single experiment (one, not three).

Notes go same-day into the pilot tracker: `Merchant · Week · Numbers · Friction (verbatim) · Experiment · Health (🟢🟡🔴)`.

## Feedback collection & routing

- **Channels:** weekly call (above) · in-app feedback modal (built — tag `pilot`) · ad-hoc LINE messages (copied into tracker).
- **Synthesis:** fortnightly one-pager to CTO: top frictions ranked by (# merchants affected × severity), each with verbatim quotes. Bugs > onboarding friction > copy confusion > feature wishes.
- **Loop closure:** when something a merchant reported ships, they get a personal LINE: "You asked, it's live." (This is what turns pilots into evangelists.)

## Support process

- Dedicated LINE thread per merchant; business-hours response <2h, same-day resolution target.
- **Every support question is first answered with a Help Center link + the direct answer.** If the manual couldn't answer it, that's a content bug — logged like a code bug.
- Weekly office hour (fixed slot) for anything hands-on; screen-share or shop visit for the stuck.

## Case studies (target 5, template)

`Shop photo → the before (paper cards / nothing, in the owner's words) → the 8-week numbers (members, repeat visits, redemptions, one win-back win) → the owner quote → segment tag + module list.`
Selection: pick across segments (2 coffee, 1 salon/nails, 1 restaurant, 1 other); include one *modest* result told honestly — believability beats perfection in this market.

## Testimonials (target 3 video + 5 written)

- Ask at the **week-6 high point** (first win-back success / 100th member), never at week-8 goodbye.
- Video: 15–30s vertical, owner at their counter, one question: "What changed since you started?" Thai, subtitled.
- Written: one quote + name + shop + photo, consent logged in LINE.

## Graduation (week 8)

Per-merchant close-out: results one-pager (their numbers vs. cohort) · plaque handover photo (content!) · plan conversation for month 7+ (target ≥12/20 continuing on paid when free period ends — tracked from day one, no surprise pricing) · the referral ask (below) · exit interview for any who leave (verbatim, logged).
Cohort close-out: full pilot report to CTO/PO — activation stats, conversion intent, top-10 frictions fixed/open, case-study pack, updated ROI defaults.

## Referral incentives

- **During pilot:** each Founding Merchant who refers a shop that activates gets +1 month free (stacking, cap 3) — pending PO ratification of the give.
- **The ask, scripted:** "Which shop owner do you know — supplier, neighbor, friend — who complains customers don't come back?" Ask at weeks 4, 6, and graduation. Warm intro (they message first) > name-drop > cold.
- Referred merchants get founder-level onboarding attention (they're the start of the 25%-referral engine — [10](./10-Sales-KPIs.md)).

## Program risks & tripwires

| Tripwire | Response |
|---|---|
| <10 recruits by end of week 3 | Stop; fix the pitch before burning more streets |
| ≥3 merchants 🔴 (no transactions) in any week | Founder visits each within 72h; pattern goes to CTO |
| Same friction reported by 5+ merchants | Product hotfix priority over everything |
| A pilot merchant quits angry | Founder handles personally; exit interview; assess blast radius on their street |
