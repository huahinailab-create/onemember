## OneMember — MKTG-002 | Confidential | 2026

---

## Overview

This script is for the live product demonstration — after the sales presentation, when the merchant has expressed interest and you want to show them the product in real time.

**Total demo time:** 12–15 minutes  
**Goal:** Enrol the merchant's first real member before you leave  
**Required:** Laptop or phone with OneMember merchant dashboard + merchant's phone (or yours as member)

---

## Pre-Demo Setup (Before the meeting)

- [ ] Open OneMember merchant dashboard on your device (use demo account or merchant's new account)
- [ ] Have the QR code ready to scan
- [ ] Know the merchant's business name, type, and average spend (for personalisation)
- [ ] Have the ROI Calculator open with their numbers prefilled

---

## The Demo in 5 Acts

---

### Act 1 — "Here's what your customers see" (3 min)

**Say:**
> "Let me show you what this actually looks like. I'm going to be your customer right now."

**Do:** Pick up your phone. Point it at the QR code displayed on the demo screen.

**Show:** The member join screen loads instantly. No app store. No download.

**Say:**
> "Your customer sees your brand — your name, your logo. They tap once to join. That's it. In 30 seconds they're in your database and you know their name."

**Pause.** Let them look.

> "Every business in your city has a QR code. Most of them lead nowhere. Yours leads to a relationship."

---

### Act 2 — "Here's what you see" (4 min)

**Say:**
> "Now let me show you what happens in your dashboard when that customer walks in."

**Do:** Switch to the merchant dashboard view.

**Show:** The member appears in the members list. Click on the member profile.

**Say:**
> "You can see their name, when they last visited, how many points they have, and how close they are to their next reward. Every customer, at a glance."

**Do:** Show the "Send message" button.

**Say:**
> "Now watch this. It's Tuesday afternoon and you're quiet. You have 87 customers who haven't been in for 30 days. You can reach all of them with one tap — a personalised offer, a birthday discount, an 'we miss you' message."

**Do:** Demonstrate creating and sending a campaign (use the demo environment — do not send a real campaign).

> "This is what 'knowing your customers' looks like. Not guessing. Knowing."

---

### Act 3 — "Here's what your rewards look like" (2 min)

**Say:**
> "I want to show you how easy it is to set up a reward. You choose: points-based or stamp card. Let's do a quick example."

**Do:** Navigate to the rewards setup section. Show the 3-step reward creation.

**Say:**
> "Step one: what triggers the reward? A number of visits, or a points threshold. Step two: what do they get? A free drink, a discount, whatever you decide. Step three: done."

**Do:** Show the completed reward appearing in the member-facing view.

> "You just set up a reward in 45 seconds. Your customers can see it every time they scan."

---

### Act 4 — "Here's what automation looks like" (2 min)

**Say:**
> "The part merchants love most is what happens automatically."

**Do:** Navigate to the automation section. Show birthday rewards, expiry reminders, win-back triggers.

**Say:**
> "Every customer who joins gets an automatic birthday reward. You don't think about it. It just goes out. A customer who hasn't been in for 60 days gets a win-back offer — automatically. You set it once and it runs."

> "You're not managing this every day. It's running while you serve customers, while you're at the market, while you sleep."

---

### Act 5 — "Let's enrol your first real member right now" (3 min)

**Say:**
> "I want you to have a real member in your database before I leave. Do you have a customer here right now — or is there someone you could call for a second?"

**If yes:** Ask the customer to scan the QR code and go through the real enrolment.

**If no:** Say "Let me be your first member — your phone number, your name. At least you'll see exactly what the member experience feels like."

**Do:** Complete the enrolment in real time.

**Say (after the member appears in the dashboard):**
> "That's it. You now have a member. That person is in your database. You can reach them tomorrow, next week, on their birthday. They're not a face you'll forget — they're a relationship you can build on."

---

## Closing the Demo

After Act 5, pause and ask:

> "What questions do you have?"

Then:

> "Are you ready to open your account? Registration takes 5 minutes. We can do it right now."

If they say yes: proceed to account setup.  
If they hesitate: go to the Objection Handling Guide.  
If they say no today: agree a follow-up date before you leave.

---

## Common Demo Problems

### "The QR code isn't scanning."
- Check lighting. Try the phone flashlight or move to a brighter area.
- If still failing, show a screenshot of the member join screen instead. "The technology works — let me show you what it looks like."

### "I don't have my phone."
- Use yours as both merchant and member for the demonstration.

### "Can I try it myself?"
- Say yes. Guide them through each screen. This is ideal — they're now using the product, not watching it.

### "Can I see what happens when a customer gets a reward?"
- Manually add enough points to trigger the reward in the demo. Show the redemption screen.
