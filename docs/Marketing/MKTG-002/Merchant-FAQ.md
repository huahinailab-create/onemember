## Getting Started

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**  
A: Your OneMember programme is live in about 10 minutes. We walk you through it step by step. Your QR code is ready the same day.

**Q: Do my customers need to download an app?**  
A: No. Customers scan a QR code with their phone camera — no app, no download, no account to create. They join your programme in about 30 seconds.

**Q: Do I need any hardware or special equipment?**  
A: No. You print or display your QR code on your counter, at your table, or anywhere visible. That's all you need.

**Q: How do customers join?**  
A: They point their camera at your QR code. A web page opens with your branding. They enter their name and phone number. Done — they're a member.

## How It Works

**Q: What types of loyalty programmes can I create?**  
A: Two types: (1) **Points programme** — customers earn points per visit or per ฿ spent. (2) **Stamp card** — customers collect stamps and earn a reward after a set number of visits.

**Q: Can I customise the rewards?**  
A: Yes. You decide what the reward is (free item, discount, gift), when it triggers, and how many points or stamps are needed.

**Q: Can I send messages to my members?**  
A: Yes. You can send targeted campaigns to all members, or filter by visit frequency, points balance, or days since last visit.

**Q: What is the birthday reward?**  
A: When a member provides their birthday, OneMember automatically sends them a personalised offer from you during their birthday month. You set the reward once; it runs automatically.

**Q: What is a win-back campaign?**  
A: When a member hasn't visited in a set number of days (you choose — e.g. 30 days), they automatically receive a message from you with an incentive to return. No manual work required.

## Data and Privacy

**Q: Who owns my member data?**  
A: You do. Your member list is yours. We do not share, sell, or use your customer data for any purpose other than operating your loyalty programme.

**Q: Is OneMember compliant with Thailand's PDPA?**  
A: Yes. OneMember is built to comply with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). We collect only what is necessary. Customers can view, update, or delete their data at any time. Consent is always explicit and documented.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: All data is stored on servers within Thailand, in compliance with local data residency requirements.

**Q: Can my customers opt out?**  
A: Yes. Members can opt out of all communications at any time. Their data is retained for programme records unless they request deletion.

## During the Programme

**Q: Can I run more than one programme at the same time?**  
A: Yes. You can run a points programme alongside promotional campaigns. Our team will advise on the best setup for your business type.

**Q: Can I see which customers are most active?**  
A: Yes. The merchant dashboard shows total members, active members, visit frequency, points balances, and campaign performance.

**Q: What happens if a customer loses their phone?**  
A: Members can re-join using the same phone number. Their history and points are restored automatically.

**Q: Can I use OneMember at multiple locations?**  
A: Multi-location support is available. Contact our team for configuration options.

## Technical

**Q: Does OneMember work on all phones?**  
A: Yes. Any smartphone with a camera and a browser (iOS or Android) can join a programme. No specific phone model or operating system required.

**Q: What if my internet goes down?**  
A: For point-of-sale check-ins, a brief internet connection is needed to record a visit. In low-connectivity situations, a short offline buffer is available. Contact support for details.

**Q: Can I integrate OneMember with my POS system?**  
A: POS integration is on our roadmap. Currently, staff manually trigger check-ins or customers self-scan. Contact us for the integration timeline.
