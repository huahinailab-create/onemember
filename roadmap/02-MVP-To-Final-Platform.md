# OneMember — MVP to Final Platform

## What "MVP" Means for OneMember

The OneMember MVP is not a prototype. It is a **fully functional, production-quality loyalty platform** that a real business can use to run a real loyalty programme with real customers.

MVP is defined by what a merchant can do on their first day, not by what the system eventually becomes.

---

## MVP Definition (Phase 1 Complete)

A merchant can:
- ✅ Register and verify their account
- ✅ Set up a loyalty programme (points or stamps)
- ✅ Add members manually or via CSV import
- ✅ Issue points for purchases
- ✅ Create rewards that members can redeem
- ✅ View their dashboard with live metrics
- ✅ Share a QR code with customers for self-service access
- ✅ Manage their subscription and billing
- ✅ Customise their settings and branding

A customer can:
- ✅ Receive a link or QR to their loyalty card
- ✅ See their current points balance
- ✅ View available rewards
- ✅ Show their QR to staff for identification

---

## Growth Platform (Phase 1 Remaining + Phase 2 Start)

After MVP, the platform expands with features that increase merchant retention and customer engagement:

| Feature | Value |
|---|---|
| Birthday bonus | Merchants reward members on their birthday → increases visit frequency |
| Point expiry | Creates urgency to visit before points expire → increases transaction frequency |
| Receipt QR linking | Removes friction for point award → increases member participation |
| PromptPay | Enables payment-triggered points → closes the attribution gap |
| POS-Lite | Replaces paper-based systems entirely → increases platform stickiness |
| Campaign analytics | Merchants understand what works → increases retention |
| Member notifications | Keeps customers engaged between visits → improves loyalty ROI |

## Network Platform (Phase 2)

The platform becomes a two-sided network when the customer wallet launches:

```
Before wallet:
  Merchant A ──── Member A (via Merchant A's portal only)
  Merchant B ──── Member B (via Merchant B's portal only)

After wallet:
  Merchant A ──┐
  Merchant B ──┼── Customer Wallet ── Customer C
  Merchant C ──┘       (all brands, one view)
```

**Network effect:** Each new merchant makes the wallet more valuable to customers. Each new wallet customer makes OneMember more valuable to new merchants.

---

## Final Platform Vision (Phase 3)

The final OneMember platform is a **commerce and loyalty network** where:

- A merchant's customer base is 100% owned by the merchant (not rented from a platform)
- A customer's complete loyalty and purchase history lives in one privacy-controlled wallet
- Direct commerce (order, pay, earn points) happens without Grab/TikTok/Lazada in between
- Data flows with consent, not extraction
- The network spans Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam, and Singapore

### What the Final Platform Looks Like

**Merchant view:**
- Unified dashboard: loyalty programme + orders + payments + analytics
- Customer segments: high-value, at-risk, birthday this week, last visit > 30 days
- Campaign tools: automated birthday offers, win-back campaigns, tier upgrades
- Direct orders: pickup, delivery, shipping — all owned channels
- Revenue insight: points liability, redemption trends, direct vs platform revenue split

**Customer view (wallet):**
- All loyalty memberships in one screen
- Points, rewards, promotions — live balance across all brands
- Order history and receipts
- Privacy control panel: what each merchant can see
- Nearby merchants with active campaigns
- Scan to join any new merchant

**Enterprise view:**
- Bridge API connecting their existing membership to OneMember wallet
- White-label portal under their own domain
- Consent-based customer data flowing from OneMember wallet into their CRM

## Non-Goals (What OneMember Will Never Be)

- **A social media platform.** OneMember facilitates loyalty relationships, not public social interactions.
- **An advertising network.** Merchants market to their own customers — we do not resell customer data to third parties.
- **A financial institution.** Points are rewards, not financial instruments. OneMember does not hold customer funds.
- **A super-app.** OneMember does one thing very well: connect loyal customers with the merchants they already love.
