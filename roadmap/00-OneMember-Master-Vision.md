# OneMember — Master Vision

## What Is OneMember?

OneMember is a **loyalty, membership, and commerce infrastructure platform** built for small and medium businesses in Southeast Asia.

It exists to give independent merchants the same tools that large chains have — without the complexity, cost, or technical expertise those tools usually require.

OneMember's design principle: **a coffee shop owner in Bangkok should be able to run a professional loyalty programme in the same afternoon they decide to start one.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

### For Merchants

Small businesses in Thailand and Southeast Asia face three compounding problems:

1. **Platform dependency** — Grab, TikTok, Lazada, and food delivery platforms charge 15–30% of every order. Merchants have no alternative sales channel and no direct relationship with their customers.

2. **No customer data ownership** — When a customer orders through a platform, the merchant never learns who they are. The platform owns the customer relationship.

3. **No affordable loyalty tools** — Enterprise loyalty platforms cost thousands of dollars per month. Free tools are either too simple or require technical setup. Most small businesses run loyalty programmes on paper stamp cards or manual WhatsApp lists.

### For Customers

1. **Wallet fragmentation** — Every brand has its own app, card, or LINE OA. A customer who shops at 10 different places manages 10 different loyalty systems.

2. **No data control** — Customers have no visibility into what data brands collect about them or how it is used.

3. **Friction** — Downloading a new app for every brand is a dealbreaker. Customers skip loyalty programmes because the process is too slow.

---

## The OneMember Solution

### For Merchants
- Set up a loyalty programme in minutes, not weeks.
- Accept members via QR scan — no app required for the customer (web-based).
- Own your customer relationships directly.
- Sell products and accept payments without platform fees.
- See meaningful analytics about your customers and campaigns.

### For Customers
- One QR scan joins any participating merchant.
- One wallet view shows all loyalty balances, rewards, and promotions.
- Explicit control over what data each merchant sees.
- Works in the browser — no app download required.

## Core Values

**Simplicity first.** If a merchant needs training to use it, it is too complex.

**Privacy by design.** Customer data is never shared without explicit consent.

**Merchant sovereignty.** We help merchants own their customer relationships, not intermediate them.

**Fair economics.** OneMember charges flat fees or low percentages — always significantly less than incumbent platforms.

**Regional roots.** Built for Southeast Asia: Thai language, PromptPay, Buddhist calendar, local market needs.

## Platform Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Loyalty Engine
The core product. Points, stamps, rewards, redemptions, birthday bonuses, campaign management. This is what makes OneMember useful from day one.

### Pillar 2 — Membership Management
Every merchant has a member database they own. Import, export, segment, communicate. The merchant's customer list is their most valuable business asset — OneMember protects and grows it.

### Pillar 3 — Customer Wallet
One universal loyalty wallet for customers. Scan once, always remembered. View all brands in one screen. Privacy-controlled data sharing.

### Pillar 4 — Direct Commerce
Order online, pick up, deliver. Merchant-controlled. No platform commission beyond a small OneMember fee. PromptPay native in Thailand.

### Pillar 5 — Enterprise Bridge
For large brands with existing membership systems. OneMember connects their members to the universal wallet ecosystem without replacing their existing infrastructure.

### Pillar 6 — Privacy & Analytics
Anonymized, consent-based purchase analytics for merchants. Aggregate insights without individual PII exposure.
