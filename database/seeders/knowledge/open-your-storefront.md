The storefront is your own product page where customers browse and order. It is part of the **Commerce app**.

1. Open **Apps** in the sidebar and press **Install** on Commerce.
2. Go to **Commerce** (now in your sidebar) and add at least one product with a photo and price.
3. Open **Fulfillment** and switch on how you hand over orders: pickup, delivery or shipping.
4. Your storefront is now live at `app.onemember.co/store/your-store-name`.

Orders go straight to you, and customers pay you directly — for example with your own payment QR, which you can upload under Commerce → Fulfillment.
