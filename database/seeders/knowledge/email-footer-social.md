Emails and customer pages can carry your contact details and social profiles.

1. Open **Settings → Business Profile**.
2. Fill in your **website**, **Facebook**, **Instagram** and **LINE** links.
3. Add a **receipt footer** line if you want one (e.g. opening hours or a thank-you note).
4. Press **Save changes**.

These appear on your join page and in member notification emails, so customers can always find you.
