The poster is how customers discover your programme at the shop.

1. Open **Launch Kit** in the sidebar.
2. Press **Poster** — it opens print-ready with your shop name and join QR.
3. Print in color on A4 if you can; the QR works in black and white too.
4. Put it where customers wait: by the register, on the counter, near the menu.

Scanning the QR opens your join page, where the customer taps to join with your staff's help.
