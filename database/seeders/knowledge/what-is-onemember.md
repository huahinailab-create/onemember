OneMember helps your shop keep customers coming back. Customers become **members**, earn **points or stamps** when they buy, and redeem **rewards** you define. You stay in full control: your prices, your rules, your customer relationships.

What you can do today:

1. Run a points or stamp **campaign**.
2. Add **members** in seconds — name and phone number is enough.
3. Create **rewards** members can redeem.
4. Print a **Launch Kit** poster so customers can join at the counter.
5. Install the **Commerce app** to take orders from your own storefront page.

Money always goes directly from your customer to you. OneMember never touches your payments and never charges commission on your sales.

Start with the checklist on your dashboard — it walks you through your first campaign, reward and member.
