1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 100 spent; visits are infrequent but tickets are large, so points beat stamps here.
2. **Rewards ladder** — 300 points: free treatment add-on · 800 points: 20% off a cut · 1500 points: free color.
3. Ask for **birthday** at sign-up and enable the **birthday reward** — a free blow-dry brings people in during their birthday month.
4. Poster at reception, counter card at the till; receptionists add members while customers wait.
