Counter Mode is the fast screen your staff use to record purchases during service.

1. Switch it on in **Settings → Business Preferences**.
2. Press the **Counter** button in the top bar.
3. Search the member by phone, name or member code.
4. Type the purchase amount and press **Record purchase** — points are added instantly.

If the customer is not a member yet, the **Add member** shortcut is right there on the empty search result.
