1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 25 spent, so even small purchases feel rewarded.
2. **Rewards** — 200 points: 10% off next purchase · 600 points: fixed-amount voucher.
3. **Import your customer list** (Settings → Data) if you kept one — your regulars start with their history of trust intact.
4. Counter card at the till; staff ask one question — "phone number for points?" — at every checkout.

Install **Commerce** to publish your catalogue; customers check stock and reserve for pickup.
