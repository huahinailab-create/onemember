title: Archive a member
sort: 3
---
Archiving hides a member from your active list without deleting anything — their history stays intact.

1. Open the member's profile page.
2. Press **Archive** and confirm.

To see archived members later, open **Members** and switch the filter at the top to **Archived**. Archived members stop appearing in searches and Counter Mode.
