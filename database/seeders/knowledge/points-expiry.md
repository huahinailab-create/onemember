---
By default points **never expire** — the simplest and friendliest setting, and what we recommend.

If you want expiry (for example points lapse after 12 months):

1. Open **Settings → Business Preferences**.
2. Under **Points expiration**, choose months or years and the duration. This becomes the default for new campaigns.

Members are warned by email before points expire, and expiries appear in the transaction history like any other movement.
