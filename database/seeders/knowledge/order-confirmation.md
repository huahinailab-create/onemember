title: What the customer sees after ordering
sort: 7
---
After placing an order the customer lands on a **confirmation page** showing their order number, items, total, and how they chose to receive it.

The same page shows **your payment instructions and payment QR** (set them under **Commerce → Fulfillment**) so they can pay you directly on the spot.

The page updates as you work: when you accept, mark paid or complete the order, the customer sees the new status by refreshing the page they were told to keep.
