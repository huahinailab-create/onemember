title: Redeem a reward
sort: 4
---
When a member has enough points or stamps:

1. Open the member's profile (search by phone in **Members** or **Counter Mode**).
2. The **Rewards** section shows what they can afford — press **Redeem** on the chosen reward.
3. Confirm. Points are deducted and the redemption is recorded in their history.

Hand over the reward and you are done. Every redemption is stored permanently, so you can always check what was redeemed and when.
