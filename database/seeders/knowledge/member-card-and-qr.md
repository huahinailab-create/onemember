title: The member card and QR code
sort: 5
---
Every member gets a personal portal page with their points, rewards and a QR code — no app download needed.

- Open a member's profile and press **View card** to see or share their card link.
- Members can save the page to their phone's home screen like a card in their wallet.
- The QR contains only a secure code — never their name or phone number.

At the counter, staff can look the member up by phone number in **Counter Mode**, or the member simply shows their card.
