---
Give members an automatic gift in their birthday month.

1. Open your campaign settings and enable the **birthday bonus**, choosing bonus points or a reward.
2. Make sure members have a **birthday** saved on their profile — ask when they sign up.

OneMember applies the birthday bonus automatically and sends the member an email. New campaigns follow the default set in **Settings → Business Preferences**.
