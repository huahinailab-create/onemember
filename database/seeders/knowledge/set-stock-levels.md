Stock tracking is optional per product.

- Leave the **stock** field empty → the product is always orderable (good for services and made-to-order items).
- Enter a number → OneMember counts down as orders come in. At zero the storefront shows **Out of stock** and customers can no longer order it.

The products list shows a stock badge for every item — including an "Only X left" warning when 5 or fewer remain. Update the number any time from the product form.
