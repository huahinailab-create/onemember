1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 100 spent, so a 60-minute and a 120-minute session earn proportionally.
2. **Rewards** — 500 points: 30-minute extension · 1000 points: free foot massage · 2000 points: full session.
3. Poster in the waiting area, counter card at checkout; reception adds members during payment.
4. Enable the **birthday reward** — spas are birthday-treat businesses; a discount voucher in their birthday month fills weekday slots.
