title: QR code not scanning
sort: 6
---
1. **Reprint fresh from the Launch Kit** — faded, laminated-with-glare or shrunken reprints are the usual culprit. Print at 100% scale, not "fit to page".
2. **Size matters** — the QR should be at least 2 × 2 cm; bigger for posters read from a distance.
3. **Light and angle** — glossy lamination under a spotlight defeats cameras; matte paper works better.
4. **Test with a normal camera app** — modern phones scan QRs natively; if yours scans but a customer's doesn't, their camera may need the code closer or further away.

The QR only contains your join link, so as a fallback the customer can type the link printed under the code.
