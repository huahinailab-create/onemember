Your branding follows your customers everywhere: join page, member portal, member cards and emails.

1. Open **Settings → Business Profile**.
2. Upload your **logo** (square PNG with transparency looks best).
3. Pick your **brand colors** — they theme the customer-facing pages.
4. Add a **tagline** and **receipt footer** if you want them shown.
5. Press **Save changes**.

Check the result by opening your own join page or a member's card — it should look unmistakably like your shop.
