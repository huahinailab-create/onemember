Rewards are what members redeem their points or stamps for.

1. Open your campaign and go to the **Rewards** tab.
2. Press **Add reward**.
3. Name it (e.g. "Free iced latte"), set the points or stamps required, and optionally limit the quantity.
4. Press **Save** and set the reward **Active**.

Good first rewards are cheap for you but exciting for customers: a free drink, a 10% discount, a small gift. Two or three rewards at different point levels work better than one big one.
