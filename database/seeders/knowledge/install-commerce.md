---
Commerce adds a product catalogue, a public storefront and order management.

1. Open **Apps** in the sidebar.
2. Press **Install** on the Commerce card.
3. **Commerce** appears in your sidebar immediately.

Commerce is optional — if you only run loyalty, you never need it. You can disable or uninstall it any time from the Apps page; your product data is kept.
