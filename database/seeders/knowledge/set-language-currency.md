title: Set your language and currency
context: settings

1. Open **Settings → Localization**.
2. Choose your **country**, **primary currency** and **timezone**.
3. Pick your **internal language** — the language your team sees in this dashboard (Thai or English today).
4. Tick the **customer-facing languages** you want to offer. Customers can switch between them on your storefront; the first one is the default.
5. Press **Save changes**.

You can also tick extra **accepted currencies** to show customers which currencies you take at the counter. Prices are never converted automatically — they always display in your primary currency.
