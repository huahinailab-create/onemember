1. Open **Commerce** and press **Add product**.
2. Fill in the sections top to bottom:
   - **General** — name and a short description (the storefront shows the first two lines).
   - **Image** — add a photo; products with photos sell noticeably better.
   - **Pricing** — the price in your currency.
   - **Inventory** — leave stock empty if you don't track it.
   - **Visibility** — *Active* shows it on the storefront, *Hidden* keeps it saved but unlisted.
3. Press **Save changes**.

Type a category name while adding products to group your storefront into sections automatically.
