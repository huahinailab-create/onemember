Stamp cards reward visits — the classic "buy 9, get 1 free".

1. Open **Campaigns** and press **Create campaign**.
2. Choose **Stamps**.
3. Set how many stamps complete the card (10 is typical).
4. Save and set the campaign **Active**.

Each qualifying purchase adds one stamp. Members see their stamp card fill up on their member portal — a strong reason to come back.
