On the product form, the **Image** section is a full photo tool:

1. Drag a photo onto the upload card, click it to browse, or take a photo on your phone.
2. A preview appears with the file's size and dimensions.
3. Press **Crop** to frame it — square (1:1) is the default and displays best; 4:5 and 16:9 are available. Rotate buttons fix sideways phone photos.
4. Press **Apply**, then save the product.

Guidelines: 1200 × 1200 px is ideal, 800 × 800 px minimum, JPG/PNG/WebP up to 2 MB. OneMember automatically optimizes every photo for fast loading.
