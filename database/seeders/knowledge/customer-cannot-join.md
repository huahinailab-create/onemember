title: Customer cannot join
sort: 3
---
1. **QR opens nothing?** See "QR code not scanning" below — usually a print-quality issue.
2. **Join page shows "not found"?** Your account may be suspended or the printed link outdated. Open the Launch Kit and compare the current QR with the printed one.
3. **Phone number rejected when staff add them?** The number is probably already a member — search Members by phone first; the customer may have joined before.
4. **Customer moved from another shop?** Use **Add via scan** on the Members page and scan their OneMember Card — details transfer with their consent.
