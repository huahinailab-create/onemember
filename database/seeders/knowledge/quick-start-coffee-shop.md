The classic setup, live in 15 minutes:

1. **Stamp campaign** — 10 stamps per card, one stamp per drink purchase.
2. **Reward** — free drink of choice at 10 stamps.
3. **Counter card** by the register; **poster** near the pickup counter.
4. Turn on **Counter Mode** — baristas record purchases by phone number in seconds.

Optional: install **Commerce** and list your beans and merchandise for pickup orders. Add a **birthday reward** (free cake slice) once the basics run smoothly.
