A complete profile makes your storefront, join page and receipts look professional.

1. Open **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. On the **Business Profile** tab, fill in your business name, contact person, phone and address.
3. Add your website and social links if you have them — they appear on customer-facing pages.
4. Press **Save changes**.

Your business name is what customers see on the join page, the storefront and every notification email, so write it exactly the way you want customers to read it.
