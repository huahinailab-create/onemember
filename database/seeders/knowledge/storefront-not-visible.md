---
If your store link shows "not found" or looks empty:

1. **Commerce installed?** Open **Apps** — Commerce must show as *Installed* (and not disabled).
2. **Link correct?** Compare against the address in this article's Store URL guide — one wrong letter gives "not found".
3. **Products active?** Only products with status **Active** appear. Hidden products don't.
4. **Ordering missing?** Customers can only order if at least one fulfillment method is on: **Commerce → Fulfillment**.
5. **Account active?** If your trial or subscription lapsed, public pages pause until it's restored.
