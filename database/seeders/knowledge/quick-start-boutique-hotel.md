1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 100 spent on rooms, restaurant and spa together, so every touchpoint earns.
2. **Rewards** — 1000 points: late checkout · 3000 points: room upgrade · 8000 points: free night.
3. Add guests as members at **check-in** — reception has their details already; it takes seconds.
4. Counter card at the front desk; poster by the elevator.

Returning direct-booking guests are worth far more than OTA guests — the programme gives them a reason to book with you directly next time.
