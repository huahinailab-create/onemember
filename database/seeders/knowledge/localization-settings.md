Everything regional lives in **Settings → Localization**:

- **Country** — where you operate; also decides which local payment options OneMember can offer you later.
- **Primary currency** — how prices display everywhere.
- **Additional accepted currencies** — informational badges for customers.
- **Timezone** — used for daily counters and reports.
- **Internal language** — your dashboard (Thai or English).
- **Customer-facing languages** — what your storefront and member pages offer; customers switch freely.

Example: a Phnom Penh shop can run the dashboard in English, show customers Khmer + English, and accept KHR + USD.
