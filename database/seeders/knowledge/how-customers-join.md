Joining takes under a minute and needs no app:

1. The customer scans your poster or counter-card QR and sees your join page — your offer, how the programme works.
2. They show the page to your staff.
3. Staff press **Add Member**, type the customer's name and phone number, done.
4. The customer gets a member card link they can save to their phone.

If the customer already uses OneMember at another shop, staff can scan their OneMember Card instead — with the customer's consent their details transfer in one tap, nothing retyped.
