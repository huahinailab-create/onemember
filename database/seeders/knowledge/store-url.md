Your public links are built from your **Store URL**, set in **Settings → Business Profile**:

- Join page: `app.onemember.co/join/your-store-name`
- Storefront: `app.onemember.co/store/your-store-name`

To change it:

1. Open **Settings → Business Profile** and find the **Store URL** field.
2. Type the new name — availability is checked while you type, and only letters, numbers and dashes are kept.
3. Save. You will be asked to confirm, because the old link stops working.
4. **Reprint your Launch Kit** — printed QR codes still point to the old address.

The full public link is shown under the field with a copy button, ready to paste into social media profiles.
