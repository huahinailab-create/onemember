sort: 2
---
Your public links are built from your store name, created at registration:

- Join page: `app.onemember.co/join/your-store-name`
- Storefront: `app.onemember.co/store/your-store-name`

They are printed into your Launch Kit QR codes automatically, so you rarely need to type them.

Changing the link is not yet self-service (it would break posters you have already printed). During the beta, contact OneMember support to change it — then reprint your Launch Kit.
