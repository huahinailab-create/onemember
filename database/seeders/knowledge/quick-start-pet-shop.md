1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 50 spent; food and supply runs are frequent and predictable.
2. **Rewards** — 300 points: free treats bag · 800 points: discount on grooming or a big food bag.
3. Record the **pet's birthday** in the member's birthday field — a birthday treat for the pet is marketing gold.
4. Install **Commerce** for repeat-purchase items (food, litter) with pickup — customers reorder from their phone and you keep 100% of the sale.
