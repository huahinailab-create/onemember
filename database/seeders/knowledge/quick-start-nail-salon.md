1. **Stamp campaign** — 1 stamp per visit, 8 stamps for the reward; nail appointments are regular and similar in value, perfect for stamps.
2. **Reward** — free classic manicure, or a gel upgrade.
3. **Counter card** at the payment desk — clients sit with their phones anyway, joining takes a minute.
4. Save each client's **birthday**: a birthday nail-art upgrade is cheap and memorable.

When ready, add **Commerce** to sell polish and care products for pickup.
