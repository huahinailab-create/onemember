---
Already have a customer list? Import it in one step.

1. Open **Settings → Data**.
2. Choose **Import members** and download the sample CSV to see the expected columns.
3. Fill your file the same way (name and phone are the important ones), then upload it.
4. Review the preview — rows with problems are shown before anything is saved.
5. Confirm the import.

Duplicate phone numbers are skipped, so it is safe to re-run an import.
