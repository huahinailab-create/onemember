Your logo appears on your join page, member portal and customer emails.

1. Open **Settings → Business Profile**.
2. Find the **Logo** section and choose an image file.
3. A preview appears immediately — check it looks right.
4. Press **Save changes**.

Tips: a square logo displays best, and PNG files with a transparent background look cleanest on colored headers. You can remove or replace the logo any time from the same place.
