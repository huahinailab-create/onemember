The counter card is a small A6 print that sits next to your register — big join QR, short call to action, your shop name.

1. Open **Launch Kit** and press **Counter card**.
2. Print it, ideally on thicker paper, and place it face-up at the point of payment.

It is the highest-converting spot in the shop: customers are already standing there with their phone out to pay.
