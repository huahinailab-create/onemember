1. **Points campaign** — 1 point per 50 spent; tickets vary too much for stamps.
2. **Rewards** — 200 points: free dessert · 500 points: free main dish.
3. **Counter card** at the cashier; staff record the bill amount in **Counter Mode** while taking payment.
4. Install **Commerce**, photograph your best sellers, and switch on **pickup and delivery** — your storefront becomes a commission-free ordering page you can share on social media.
