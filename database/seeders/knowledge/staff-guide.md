Your team makes the programme work. The **Staff Guide** in the Launch Kit is a one-page printout covering the five moments that matter:

1. Searching a member by phone number.
2. Adding a new member in under 30 seconds.
3. Recording a purchase.
4. Redeeming a reward.
5. Using Counter Mode during rush hour.

Print one copy per station and walk the team through it once — that is genuinely all the training the counter flow needs.
