When you register, OneMember creates a web address for your shop from your business name. You get two public pages:

- **Join page** — `app.onemember.co/join/your-store-name` — where customers learn about your loyalty programme.
- **Storefront** — `app.onemember.co/store/your-store-name` — your product page (needs the Commerce app).

The easiest way to share the join page is the QR poster in the **Launch Kit** — it already contains your link.

During the private beta the store link cannot be changed from Settings. If your link needs correcting, contact OneMember support and we will change it for you.
