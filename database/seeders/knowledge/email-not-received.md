---
For verification, reset or notification emails that never arrive:

1. **Check spam/junk** first — then mark OneMember as "not spam" so the next one lands normally.
2. **Check the address** on the account or member profile — a one-letter typo is the most common cause.
3. **Wait two minutes** — email can queue briefly during busy periods.
4. For a member's notification: confirm the member actually has an email saved; phone-only members don't receive email.

Still nothing? Contact support with the address and the approximate time — we can see the delivery log.
