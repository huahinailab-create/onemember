Your storefront address is `app.onemember.co/store/your-store-name` — open it in any browser, no login needed, exactly as customers see it.

What appears there:

- Your business name, contact details and pickup/delivery badges.
- Your loyalty programme and top rewards, with a link to join.
- All **Active** products, grouped by category, with photos and prices.

If something looks wrong, check the product is set to **Active** and has a photo, and that fulfillment is switched on under **Commerce → Fulfillment**.
