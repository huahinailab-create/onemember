When a customer orders from your storefront:

1. The order appears in **Commerce → Orders** under the **Open** tab with the customer's name, phone and items.
2. Press **Accept** to confirm you are preparing it, then move it along with the action buttons as you fulfil it.
3. When the customer pays you (cash, transfer or your payment QR), press **Mark paid**.
4. Finish with **Complete** — the order moves to the Completed tab.

You can cancel an order at any step before completion. Call the customer using the phone number on the order if anything needs checking.
