1. Open **Members** in the sidebar and press **Add Member**.
2. Enter the customer's **name** and **mobile number** — that is all you need. Email and birthday are optional (birthday unlocks birthday rewards).
3. Press **Save**.

The member starts earning immediately: record their first purchase from their member page or from **Counter Mode**.

Faster options: if the customer already has a OneMember Card from another shop, use **Add via scan** on the Members page — with their consent their details fill in automatically.
