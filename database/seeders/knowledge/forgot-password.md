title: Forgot password
sort: 4
---
1. On the login page, press **Forgot your password?**
2. Enter the email you registered with and send the request.
3. Open the email and follow the reset link — it expires after a short time, so use it promptly.
4. Choose a new password (at least 12 characters with upper/lowercase, a number and a symbol).

No email after a few minutes? Check spam, and confirm you typed the address you actually registered with — the form won't reveal whether an email exists, for security.
