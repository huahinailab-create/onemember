title: Product image not showing
sort: 1
---
Work through these in order:

1. **Re-open the product** — does the Image section show a current photo? If not, the upload didn't finish. Upload again and wait for the preview before saving.
2. **Check the file** — JPG, PNG or WebP only, maximum 2 MB. The form tells you if a file was rejected.
3. **Hard-refresh the storefront** (Ctrl+Shift+R / pull-to-refresh) — your browser may show an old cached page.
4. Still blank? Replace the image with a fresh export of the photo — occasionally a file is corrupted by the camera app.

If none of that helps, contact support with the product name.
